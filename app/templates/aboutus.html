About Our App

Music Like is a Web Application That helps search for Music Recommendations.

Our Mission

At Music Like, we are passionate about music and technology.
Our mission is to provide music enthusiasts with a platform where they can discover
new music, create personalized playlists, and connect with other music lovers from around the world.


What We Offer

Discover New Music

Explore our vast collection of tracks spanning various genres, artists, and albums.
With our intuitive search and recommendation features, you can easily find the perfect song for any mood or occasion.

Search Personalized Songs

Search your favorite tracks by searching your favorite tracks or artists.
Whether you're into upbeat pop hits, soothing jazz melodies, or hard-hitting rock anthems,
Music Like allows you to search songs that reflect your unique taste in music.

Our Team

Music Like was founded by a team of dedicated music lovers and tech enthusiasts
who share a common goal of bringing the joy of music to people worldwide.
Our team is committed to continuously improving the Music Like platform and providing users
with the best possible music discovery experience.
